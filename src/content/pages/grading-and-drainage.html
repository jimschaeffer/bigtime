Prevent Erosion & Water Damage with Professional Grading & Drainage

At Big Time Lawn, we provide professional grading and drainage services to protect your property from standing water, soil erosion, and foundation issues. Poor drainage can lead to costly damage, while improper grading can cause water to pool in unwanted areas. Our expert team ensures proper land leveling and efficient drainage solutions, creating a more stable and functional landscape for your home.

The Importance of Proper Grading & Drainage

Without proper grading, water can collect around your home’s foundation, weaken your lawn, and create muddy patches that make your yard difficult to maintain. Our grading services reshape your land to direct water flow away from structures and low-lying areas, while our drainage systems efficiently manage excess water.

Signs Your Property Needs Grading & Drainage Services

Not sure if your yard has drainage issues? Look out for these common signs that indicate a need for professional grading and drainage solutions:

  • Pooled Water: After rain, water collects in low areas instead of draining properly.
  • Soil Erosion: Visible dirt displacement or washed-out areas on your property.
  • Foundation Issues: Water pooling near your home’s foundation, leading to cracks or damage.
  • Muddy or Soggy Lawn: Sections of your yard remain excessively wet long after rainfall.
  • Basement or Crawlspace Moisture: Excess humidity or leaks caused by poor exterior drainage.

Our Grading & Drainage Services

We provide a full range of grading and drainage solutions to improve your property’s stability and water management. Our services include:

  1. Land Grading: Reshaping and leveling your yard to create a smooth, well-drained surface.
  2. French Drains: Installing underground drainage pipes to channel excess water away from your home.
  3. Swale Installation: Creating shallow ditches to direct water flow and prevent pooling.
  4. Dry Creek Beds: Decorative and functional rock-lined channels that improve drainage.
  5. Gutter & Downspout Drainage: Redirecting roof runoff safely away from your home’s foundation.
  6. Retaining Walls: Constructing barriers that prevent soil erosion and manage sloped areas.
