What is Sod Installation?

Sod installation involves laying pre-grown grass onto prepared soil, creating a mature, green lawn without the wait of traditional seeding. Our team handles every step of the process, from soil preparation to installation, ensuring a seamless and long-lasting result. With high-quality sod sourced from trusted suppliers, we guarantee a lawn that is both beautiful and durable.

Benefits of Choosing Sod for Your Lawn

Sod is the perfect solution for homeowners looking to quickly transform their outdoor space. Here’s why sod installation is an excellent investment:

  • Instant Results: Enjoy a fully grown lawn the moment installation is complete.
  • Prevents Soil Erosion: Sod stabilizes soil immediately, making it ideal for sloped or erosion-prone areas.
  • Thick, Weed-Free Coverage: High-quality sod creates a dense, uniform surface that naturally resists weeds.
  • Healthier Lawn: Professionally grown sod establishes faster and is less susceptible to pests and diseases.

How to Care for Your New Sod

Proper aftercare is essential to help your new sod establish roots and grow successfully. Follow these tips for the best results:

  1. Water Regularly: Keep the sod and soil moist for the first two weeks, watering daily to encourage root growth.
  2. Avoid Heavy Foot Traffic: Stay off the sod until it’s firmly rooted to prevent damage.
  3. Fertilize Appropriately: Use a balanced fertilizer to promote healthy growth and a lush appearance.
  4. Mow at the Right Time: Wait until the grass reaches 3-4 inches before mowing for the first time.
